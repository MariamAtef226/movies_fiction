And Then There Were None:
What is this Website?

Have you ever thought while reading a book, who would play the role of this or that character? or have you ever watched an adpatation and told yourself "Nah, Julia Roberts would've been more sutiable for this one"? Then this website is the right pleace for you!
Dive in our library, see our own castings and tell us abour yours. Let us staurate our imagination!

About the founder:

I'm Mariam, a 21-year-old Computer Engineering student from Alexandria's University in Egypt. I've always been an imaginative person. Whenever I read a novel, I had to set the cast to play the movie adaptaion for this novel first, then I start reading, so that I imaginate the events best. Smiliraly, whenever I watch any movie or show, my mind automatically starts to create a sequel or another story based on what I've watched. I've grown up like that and I know that many of you is like that as well. So I've made this site for all of us to share our imagination and thoughts together. Have fun, imaginators!
